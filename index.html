The Studio

Architecture as
craft & conviction

I'm Jill Kostolanyi, a licensed architect based in Fargo, North Dakota. My practice spans residential design, commercial architecture, and short-term rental transformations — work where spatial quality translates directly into lived experience and investment returns.

Every project begins with a thorough listening process. I believe the best buildings emerge from deep collaboration between architect, client, and site — where program, material, and light come together with intention.
